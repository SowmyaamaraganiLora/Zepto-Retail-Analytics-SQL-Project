# Zepto-Retail-Analytics-SQL-Project
## Project Overview

This project analyzes Zepto's retail inventory dataset using SQL to uncover insights related to pricing, discounts, stock availability, inventory management, and revenue generation.

The objective is to help business stakeholders identify revenue opportunities, optimize inventory allocation, reduce stockouts, and improve overall retail performance.

---

## Business Problems Solved

1. Top revenue-generating categories
2. Best-value products based on discounts
3. High MRP products currently out of stock
4. Revenue contribution by category
5. Inventory weight analysis
6. Stockout risk assessment
7. Pricing anomaly detection
8. Premium product identification
9. Category-wise discount analysis
10. Inventory efficiency evaluation

## Key Insights

- Top-performing categories generated the majority of revenue.
- Several premium products were unavailable due to stockouts.
- A small percentage of products contributed a significant portion of revenue.
- Certain categories carried excessive inventory with lower revenue impact.

---

## Business Recommendations

1. Prioritize replenishment of high-value stockout products.
2. Increase inventory allocation to top-performing categories.
3. Review discount strategies for low-performing products.
4. Improve inventory forecasting and monitoring.
5. Optimize warehouse utilization based on revenue contribution.
